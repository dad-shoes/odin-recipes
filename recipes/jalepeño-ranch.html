Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 4 Tbsp cilantro, chopped
  • 1 jalapeno, Seeded and chopped (2 Tbsp)
  • 2 tsp lime juice, freshly squeezed
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder

Instructions:

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine sour cream, cilantro, jalapeno, lime juice, salt, onion powder, and garlic powder.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary.
  4. Transfer to a serving bowl or container.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to let the flavors meld together.
  6. Serve chilled with your favorite dishes or as a dip.
